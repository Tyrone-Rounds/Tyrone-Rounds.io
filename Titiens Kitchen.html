About us

With over 10 years of experience catering, Tittien's Kitchen has a mix of American and traditional Indonesian dishes that is sure to satisfy the tastes of most clients in the Yogyakarta area. We cater all types of events throughout the Yogyakarta area such as: weddings, birthdays, holidays, business meetings, or any other special event. Titien Rahayu is the main cook. She has traveled the world, and is expert at cooking a mix of American, and traditional Indonesian dishes.
